A few years before, my boss acquired an old broken arcade machine during a store liquidation, and I was entrusted with the exciting task of bringing it back to life. The primary objective was not only to restore its gaming functionality but also to transform it into a captivating tool for marketing, self-presentation, and showcasing company information.

The final implementation featured an integrated screen with a custom application that showcased all the company's projects and presented the individuals behind them. Utilizing the machine's original arcade buttons for control added an authentic touch to the experience. Additionally, we incorporated a game mode that allowed users to choose from a variety of classic arcade games, successfully preserving the machine's nostalgic gaming features.

To achieve this transformation, I spend considerable time and in physical prototyping. I connected all the buttons with an Arduino, ensuring reliable input recognition and integrated a new screen to the machine. Furthermore I developed a software solution that seamlessly processed the data input from the Arduino. I established a connection to the gaming emulator, and implementing an the desired user interface design.

The end result was an immersive and visually appealing experience that captivated users while effectively conveying the company's projects and information.
