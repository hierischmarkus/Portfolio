Flutter180 is a simple dart counter app that could be accessed across multiple platforms.

Our team implemented an application that allows users to create multiple profiles and track the scoring of a live dart game within the app. Additionally, the app provides basic statistics to compare different players and measure their progress. To ensure maximum accessibility, the app was designed as a Progressive Web Application that can be installed on mobile devices, giving users a similar experience to native apps.

Throughout the project, I was involved in the planning and conception process. During the development phase, I was responsible for creating some of the application's features, as well as developing the game logic.
