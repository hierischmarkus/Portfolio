Tegernsee Phantastisch combines two contrasting worlds and presents itself to visitors as both tradition-conscious and future-oriented. The »Tegernsee World« area combines the transfer of knowledge about nature and the region around Lake Tegernsee with fun and games. With its futuristic and energetic atmosphere, on the other hand, »Tegernsee Magic« makes every adrenaline-savvy heart beat faster. The customized installations merge real and digital spaces and create experiences for all senses that will be remembered.

"Our company created various interactive installations for this adventure park. The ones I was involved in will be presented as follows:

Zauberwald:
The enchanted world of the Bavarian forest is brought to life with the help of a mirror head projector showing scenes that appear sometimes here, sometimes there. Animated stories from the everyday life of the animals in the forest are shown in the form of funny anecdotes. I was responsible for controlling the movement of the projector, as well as converting its output to real-world space and eliminating natural distortions. Additionally, I created the basic structure of the application.

Hoehenweg:
This is an indoor adventure park The adrenaline kick is guaranteed in the high air, even more so because the colorful animations of the ground projection create the impression of the floor being even further away. During the development phase, I created numerous visual prototypes and also took charge of designing the basic structure of the application. Additionally, I implemented some of the designs myself.

Ninja Warrior:
This Ninja warriour parcour provides an experience with start management, important auditory cues and timing are controlled autonomously by means of sensor technology and gameplay logic, creating equal conditions and fair play for every warrior. The energetic visual language of the projections along the track provides an additional motivational boost. In addition, spectators can follow the sports competition from the side and take a look at the visualized section and overall times. I was responsible for facilitating the communication between the distance sensors and the application to ensure a smooth gameplay.

Hi Hupfer:
At first glance, this area seems like a normal trampoline park - but there is much more hiding behind it - or rather underneath it. Responsive Spaces developed a special sensor framework that uses ultrasound to measure in real time which trampolines are being jumped on. The dynamic and colorful light installations on the ceiling and under the trampolines take the guests into a pulsating world full of energy and action. The space is constantly reshaped by bouncing, as both the color and intensity of the lights react dynamically. Synchronous jumping on several trampolines is rewarded with a special light show. I took on the task of developing the core framework of the application, which involved creating its basic structure. Additionally, I implemented the logic for synchronized jumps and incorporated various light effects beneath the trampolines. Moreover, I established the communication system between the application and the ceiling lights above. Check out the Website of Responsive Spaces for more information and to watch the video about the project.
